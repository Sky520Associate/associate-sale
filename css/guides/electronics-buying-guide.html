How to Choose the Right Laptop

When selecting a laptop, consider these key factors:

  • Processor: Intel Core i5/i7 or AMD Ryzen 5/7 for most users
  • RAM: Minimum 8GB, 16GB recommended for future-proofing
  • Storage: SSD for better performance, 512GB minimum
